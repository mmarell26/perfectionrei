Our Buy Box
What we’re actively looking for.

Asset Type & Size

4–20 unit multifamily. Mixed-use with solid residential over retail considered. Parking and storage are a plus.

Location

Greater Philadelphia with a focus on North, West, and working-class submarkets. Strong transit, employment, or anchor-institution drivers.

How We Work
Clear, disciplined process.

Every deal follows the same rhythm: fast response, honest feedback, and straightforward next steps — even if the answer is “not a fit.”

Step 01
Quick Intake

Send the address, unit mix, current rents, and asking price. If you have photos, a rent roll, or OM, even better.

Step 02
Underwriting & Feedback

We underwrite the deal quickly and tell you where we are: strong fit, edge case, or pass — and why.

Step 03
Offer & Next Steps

If it works, we structure an offer that respects your time and our numbers. Clean terms, clear expectations, and a plan to get to the finish line.

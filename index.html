Our Buy Box

What we’re actively hunting for

If you’re a broker, wholesaler, or owner and your property fits this lane, we want to hear from you. If it’s close, send it — we’ll underwrite and respond.

Property & Location

  • 4–20 units (will review larger on a case-by-case basis).
  • Philadelphia metro, with emphasis on North, West, and surrounding neighborhoods with strong rental demand.
  • Brick or masonry preferred; newer mechanicals a plus but not required.
  • Both tenant-occupied and partially vacant considered.

Financials & Deal Profile

  • Value-add and light repositioning — we like solving problems, not buying perfection.
  • Section 8 or strong workforce housing rent potential.
  • Clear path to refinance or stable long-term hold.
  • Realistic asking price based on income and condition, not just “what someone got down the street.”
How We Work

Straightforward, grown-man process

No games, no fake urgency. Clear communication, real timelines, and disciplined follow-through — from first call to closing table.

Step 01
Intro Call or Form

You share basic deal info: address, unit count, current rents, and your goal (sell, partner, or invest).

Step 02
Underwriting & Walkthrough

We run numbers, tour the property, and confirm assumptions around condition, tenants, and upside.

Step 03
Offers & Gameplan

We present clear scenarios — all cash, financing, or partnership — with honest pros and cons.

Step 04
Execution & Close

Once you choose a path, we push to close: paperwork, lenders, title, and handoff done with respect.
